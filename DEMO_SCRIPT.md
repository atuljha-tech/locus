# 🎬 SplitEase — Video Demo Script

**Target runtime: 2.5–3 minutes.** Judges have 3–5 min per project. Stay tight.

---

## Before recording

```bash
npm run dev          # confirm localhost:3000 loads
```

- Browser: full screen, zoom 90%, dark mode OFF
- Open at **landing page** — demo data NOT loaded yet
- Close all other tabs

---

## Scene 1 — Landing Page (25 sec)

**Show:** Hero headline → animated counters → escalation ladder cycling → 3 feature cards

**Say:** *"SplitEase solves the problem everyone has — friends who don't pay back. But it does it with three features you haven't seen before."*

**Do:** Click **"Load Demo Data"** → wait for ✅ toast → click **"Open App →"**

---

## Scene 2 — Dashboard Overview (20 sec)

**Show:** Sidebar nav → greeting → 4 stat cards → live scrolling ticker

**Say:** *"The dashboard shows everything at a glance. One overdue debt, late fees accumulating, and a live activity feed."*

---

## Scene 3 — Debts + AI Shame ← MAIN DEMO (50 sec)

**Do:** Click **"Debts"** in sidebar

**Show:**
1. Three debts with colored left borders — green (paid), amber (pending), red (overdue)
2. Click **Jamie's overdue debt** to expand
3. Point to the shame meter — "Roast" level
4. Click **"🔥 Send Shame"**

**Say:** *"Jamie owes $64 for dinner. 3 days overdue. Watch what the AI sends."*

*(sound plays, toast appears with message)*

**Say:** *"That's Tier 3 — scorched earth. The escalation is automatic: gentle at T+0, snarky at T+24h, passive-aggressive at T+48h, and Claude-generated chaos at T+72h."*

---

## Scene 4 — Optimizer ← CS DEPTH MOMENT (25 sec)

**Do:** Click **"Optimizer"** in sidebar

**Show:** Net balances → optimal transfers list → savings badge

**Say:** *"Instead of everyone paying everyone else, the optimizer computes net balances and finds the minimum transfers needed. Three debts become two payments. O(n log n), provably minimal."*

---

## Scene 5 — Add Debt (15 sec)

**Do:** Click **"Add Debt"** → fill in: Jamie → You → $25 → "Taxi home" → submit

*(success sound plays)*

**Say:** *"Adding a debt takes five seconds."*

---

## Scene 6 — NFT of Shame ← THE MOMENT (30 sec)

**Do:** Click **"Debts"** → expand Jamie's debt → click **"🎨 Mint NFT"**

*(sci-fi sound plays, purple NFT card appears)*

**Say:** *"After three days overdue, we mint a soulbound, non-transferable NFT of Shame. The token ID, the transaction hash — permanently on the blockchain."*

*(pause)*

**Say:** *"There is no escape."*

---

## Scene 7 — Wall of Shame (15 sec)

**Do:** Click **"Wall of Shame 🔥"** → show /shame page → click "Share shame →"

**Say:** *"The Wall of Shame is public. This link can be sent directly to the person who owes you."*

---

## Scene 8 — Hall of Fame (10 sec)

**Do:** Click **"Hall of Fame"** in sidebar

**Say:** *"And for the people who actually pay — they get a gold medal."*

---

## Closing (10 sec)

**Do:** Navigate back to landing page

**Say:** *"SplitEase. Split smarter, collect faster. Thank you."*

---

## Recording tips

- **Mac:** QuickTime → New Screen Recording
- **Windows:** OBS Studio (free) or Win+G
- Cursor: slow, deliberate movements
- After each click: pause 1 second before speaking
- Total: aim for 2:45–3:00 exactly
